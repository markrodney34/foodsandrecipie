Moroccan Chicken Tagine.

INGREDIENTS
  1. 1 pound boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces 1 tablespoon olive oil 1 onion, chopped 2 cloves garlic, minced 1 teaspoon ground ginger 1 teaspoon ground coriander 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin 1/4 teaspoon turmeric 1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper 1/4 teaspoon salt 1/4 teaspoon black pepper 1/4 cup dried apricots, chopped 1/4 cup dried prunes, chopped 1/4 cup almonds, toasted and chopped 1 (14-ounce) can diced tomatoes, undrained 1/4 cup chicken broth 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsle
DIRECTIONS
  1. Heat the olive oil in a tagine or a large Dutch oven over medium heat. Add the onion and cook until softened, about 5 minutes. Add the garlic, ginger, coriander, cumin, turmeric, cayenne pepper, salt, and pepper and cook for 1 minute more, until fragrant. Add the chicken to the tagine and cook, stirring occasionally, until the chicken is browned on all sides. Stir in the apricots, prunes, almonds, diced tomatoes, chicken broth, and parsley. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to low and cover. Simmer for 1 hour, or until the chicken is cooked through. Serve the chicken tagine over couscous or rice.
